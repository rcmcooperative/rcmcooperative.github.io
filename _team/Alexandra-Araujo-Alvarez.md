Alexandra is Senior Research Community Manager for [BridgeAI at The Alan Turing Institute](https://www.turing.ac.uk/partnering-turing/current-partnerships-and-collaborations/innovateukbridgeai), where she supports UK organisations, particularly SMEs, to adopt and benefit from AI. She joined the Institute in 2023 as Research Project Manager for [The Turing Way](https://book.the-turing-way.org), where she supported the launch of the [Practitioners Hub](https://www.turing.ac.uk/turing-way-practitioners-hub) and contributed to the project professionalising infrastructure and research roles in data science.

Before moving into research, Alexandra led as CEO of a 250-staff theatre production company and subsequently managed research projects on mental health and wellbeing across the UK and Global South. She has also founded an award-winning SME in London. This breadth of experience, spanning community building, organisational leadership, and research practice, informs her approach to cooperative development. 

She is also a mentor for [Laboratoria+](https://laboratoria.la), supporting the professional leadership of women across Latin America.
